A Reliable Insurance Plan

For many families, solving the puzzle of how to handle the costs of assisted living and other long term care services can be almost as complicated as selecting the right provider or community.

How do you decide where the money comes from for assisted living? What happens to savings or a pension if something happens to the stock market? Is there a risk you might outlive your savings?

How Can We Help?

You can choose as little as $1,000.00 a month with higher benefits up to 110% of the monthly cost.

Other Insurance product distributions are subject to federal income taxes, we are not.

If care is needed later in life, your Reliable Living Plan benefits will be available to you.

You have 30 days to review the plan to ensure that you will be happy with the benefits.

Refund all premiums to the loved ones you designate if they pass away within the first six months.

We understand that you are just taking a look at this point. There is no obligation to sign up.

More About the Plan

The plan gives you the ability to set up ongoing monthly benefits … tax-free … that continue for as long as assisted living or other long term care services, like nursing home care, are needed.

It’s a peace of mind solution to the puzzle of paying for assisted living or other long term care services.

This innovative long-term care insurance plan helps families better plan and budget for the costs of assisted living and other types of services.

Choose Your Benefit Amount

With the Reliable Living Plan, you’re in control of the monthly benefit you select. This helps you create a solution that fits both your health situation and your family’s financial situation.

If you’re looking for a solution to reliably help cover assisted living costs for the rest of your life, the Reliable Living Plan can deliver those benefits.

If your family is comfortable paying a portion of the assisted living costs each month, you can choose a Reliable Living Plan benefit amount to cover the remaining portion.

IMPORTANT: No matter which benefit amount you choose, you can add more coverage later if you’d like.
